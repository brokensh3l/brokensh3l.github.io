How Does it Work?

To use Virtual Reality you will need a VR Headset. This is a headset with a (hopefully somewhat powerful) computer inside that displays its contents straight to your eyes and ears to create a 3d experience. There are gyroscopic sensors, accelerators, and magnetometers in the headsets to determine how you move and track your interactions within a virtual space.
